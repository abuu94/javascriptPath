Poblano Recipes

They're typically dark green and resemble other varieties of peppers, but they tend to be larger than jalape√±os and smaller than bell peppers.

Ingredients

  • 12 dried ancho chiles.
  • 12 dried guajillo chiles.
  • 6 dried pasilla chiles.
  • 4 tablespoons sesame seeds.
  • 1 teaspoon aniseed.
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns.
  • 1/2 teaspoon whole cloves.
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
  2. Rinse and pat dry poblano peppers and place on a baking sheet.
  3. Turning occasionally, roast in oven for 20-25 minutes until all sides are charred and blistered.
  4. Peppers can also be roasted on an open flame or on a griddle.
