Tomatoes Recipes

The tomato fruit is globular or ovoid. Botanically, the fruit exhibits all of the common characteristics of berries; a simple fleshy fruit that encloses its seed in the pulp.

Ingredients

  • Folate.
  • Vitamin C.
  • Potassium.

Steps

  1. Boil the tomatoes for 30 to 60 seconds.
  2. Watch carefully as smaller tomatoes will need less time.
  3. You don't want the tomatoes to start cooking.
  4. You'll know the tomatoes are ready to be removed when the flesh starts to wrinkle, and the skin starts to peel away from the flesh.
